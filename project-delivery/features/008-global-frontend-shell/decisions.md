# Decisions: Global Frontend Shell

- **Aesthetic Direction:** Decided on the "Industrial Intelligence Console" (Approach A from brainstorm) over an editorial layout to emphasize the platform's role as a high-end decision support tool.
- **Typography:** Avoided generic fonts (Inter, Arial) in favor of `Bricolage Grotesque` (authoritative) and `JetBrains Mono` (technical).
- **Styling:** Adhering strictly to sharp 1px borders, zero soft shadows, and deep black backgrounds to avoid "AI slop" aesthetics.
