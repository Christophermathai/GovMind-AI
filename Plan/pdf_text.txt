Project Proposal
GovMind AI
AI-Powered Public Knowledge, Rights & Compliance Platform
Abstract
Millions of citizens, students, entrepreneurs, workers, tenants, and small business owners lose
opportunities every year because they cannot easily access or understand government information.
Government schemes, regulations, labor laws, tax guidelines, environmental compliance documents, and
legal contracts are distributed across hundreds of websites, thousands of PDF documents, circulars,
notifications, and acts.
Although this information is publicly available, it is often difficult to search, understand, compare, and apply
to an individual's specific situation.
GovMind AI aims to solve this information accessibility problem using Retrieval-Augmented Generation
(RAG), Knowledge Graphs, Rule-Based Reasoning, and AI Agents.
Rather than functioning as another chatbot, GovMind AI acts as an intelligent decision-support platform
that continuously ingests official government information, structures it into searchable knowledge,
determines user eligibility for schemes and regulations, analyzes legal documents, assists businesses with
compliance, and provides transparent answers with complete source citations.
Problem Statement
Public information exists.
Public understanding does not.
Today, people face numerous challenges:
Government schemes are scattered across departments.
Citizens do not know which schemes they qualify for .
Small businesses struggle to understand compliance requirements.
Employment contracts are signed without understanding hidden clauses.
Rental agreements contain confusing legal language.

Environmental compliance is difficult for MSMEs.

Ethical Principles
The platform should
Always cite official sources.
Clearly distinguish retrieved facts from AI-generated explanations.
Indicate confidence where appropriate.
Encourage users to verify critical decisions with the relevant authority or qualified professionals.
Protect user privacy and personal data.
Avoid providing personalized legal or financial advice as a substitute for professionals.
Social Impact
GovMind AI aims to
Increase awareness of public benefits.
Improve government transparency.
Help MSMEs comply with regulations.
Reduce information inequality.
Improve access to legal knowledge.
Encourage informed decision-making.
Save time and resources for citizens and businesses.
Long-Term Vision
GovMind AI is not intended to be another chatbot.
It is intended to become a trusted AI-powered public knowledge infrastructure that transforms government
information, legal rights, and regulatory requirements into knowledge that is understandable, personalized,
explainable, and actionable for every citizen and every business.
